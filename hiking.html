Scenic Hiking - Darjeeling

Location:

Darjeeling is a charming hill station located in the northern part of West Bengal, India. It is renowned for its tea gardens, breathtaking views of the Himalayas, and colonial-era architecture.

Nearby Attractions:

  • Tiger Hill: Famous for its spectacular sunrise views over Mount Kanchenjunga.
  • Batasia Loop: A spiral railway track with a beautiful garden, offering panoramic views.
  • Darjeeling Himalayan Railway: A UNESCO World Heritage Site, also known as the Toy Train.
  • Peace Pagoda: A Buddhist stupa with serene surroundings and stunning views.
  • Himalayan Mountaineering Institute: A museum and training center dedicated to mountaineering.

Mode of Travel:

  • Nearest Airport: Bagdogra Airport, approximately 70 km from Darjeeling.
  • Nearest Railway Station: New Jalpaiguri Railway Station, well connected to major cities.
  • Bus Stations: Regular bus and taxi services are available from Siliguri to Darjeeling.

Overall Travelling Expenses:

  • Accommodation: Budget guesthouses range from ₹1,500 to ₹3,000 per night. Mid-range hotels cost between ₹4,000 to ₹7,000 per night, while luxury hotels can go upwards of ₹10,000 per night.
  • Food: Casual dining can cost around ₹300 to ₹800 per meal. Mid-range restaurants might charge between ₹1,000 to ₹2,000 for a meal for two, while fine dining can exceed ₹3,000.
  • Transportation: Local transport includes taxis, shared jeeps, and the Toy Train. Trekking routes are also popular.
  • Activities: Entry fees to attractions and charges for activities such as trekking, sightseeing, and tea garden tours. Budget for around ₹1,000 to ₹3,000 per day for activities.

Adventure Hiking - Kalsubai

Location:

Kalsubai is the highest peak in Maharashtra, India, located in the Western Ghats. It is a popular trekking destination offering stunning views and a challenging hike.

Nearby Attractions:

  • Kalsubai Peak: The main attraction, known for its trekking trail and panoramic views from the summit.
  • Bhandardara: A nearby village known for its lakes, waterfalls, and camping sites.
  • Arthur Lake: A serene lake surrounded by lush greenery, ideal for picnics and boating.
  • Ratangad Fort: An ancient fort offering scenic views and a popular trekking destination.
  • Wilson Dam: A historic dam offering a picturesque setting and scenic beauty.

Mode of Travel:

  • Nearest Airport: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport in Mumbai, approximately 150 km from Kalsubai.
  • Nearest Railway Station: Igatpuri Railway Station, about 50 km from Kalsubai.
  • Bus Stations: Regular bus services are available from Mumbai and Nashik to Bari village, the base village for the trek.

Overall Travelling Expenses:

  • Accommodation: Budget guesthouses and homestays in Bari village range from ₹800 to ₹1,500 per night. Camping options are also available.
  • Food: Casual dining can cost around ₹200 to ₹500 per meal. Local dhabas and eateries provide affordable options.
  • Transportation: Local transport includes shared jeeps and private vehicles from Igatpuri to Bari village.
  • Activities: Entry fees to attractions and charges for activities such as trekking and camping. Budget for around ₹500 to ₹2,000 per day for activities.
