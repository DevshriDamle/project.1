Luxury Cruise - Mumbai

Location:

Mumbai, the bustling capital city of Maharashtra, is a major hub for luxury cruises in India. It offers an excellent mix of modernity, colonial architecture, and scenic coastal views.

Nearby Attractions:

  • Gateway of India: A historic monument and the starting point for many cruise ships.
  • Marine Drive: A scenic boulevard along the coast, perfect for evening strolls.
  • Elephanta Caves: Ancient rock-cut temples located on Elephanta Island, a short ferry ride away.
  • Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus: A UNESCO World Heritage Site and an architectural marvel.
  • Juhu Beach: A popular beach with vibrant street food stalls and entertainment.

Mode of Travel:

  • Nearest Airport: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport, well connected to major cities.
  • Nearest Railway Station: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus (CSMT) and Mumbai Central Railway Station.
  • Bus Stations: Extensive bus services connect Mumbai to other parts of Maharashtra and nearby states.

Overall Travelling Expenses:

  • Accommodation: Budget hotels range from ₹2,000 to ₹5,000 per night. Mid-range hotels cost between ₹6,000 to ₹10,000 per night, while luxury hotels can go upwards of ₹15,000 per night.
  • Food: Casual dining can cost around ₹500 to ₹1,500 per meal. Mid-range restaurants might charge between ₹2,000 to ₹4,000 for a meal for two, while fine dining can exceed ₹5,000.
  • Transportation: Local transport includes taxis, auto-rickshaws, and the Mumbai local train network.
  • Activities: Entry fees to attractions and charges for activities such as sightseeing cruises and guided tours. Budget for around ₹2,000 to ₹5,000 per day for activities.

Exotic Cruise - Goa

Location:

Goa, a popular tourist destination on the western coast of India, is famous for its beaches, vibrant nightlife, and scenic cruises along its rivers and coastline.

Nearby Attractions:

  • Baga Beach: One of the most famous beaches in Goa, known for its lively atmosphere.
  • Fort Aguada: A 17th-century Portuguese fort offering stunning views of the Arabian Sea.
  • Dudhsagar Waterfalls: A majestic four-tiered waterfall located on the Mandovi River.
  • Church of Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception: A historic church in Panaji, known for its colonial architecture.
  • Spice Plantations: Take a tour of the spice plantations and learn about various spices grown in Goa.

Mode of Travel:

  • Nearest Airport: Dabolim Airport, well connected to major cities in India and abroad.
  • Nearest Railway Station: Madgaon (Margao) Railway Station and Thivim Railway Station.
  • Bus Stations: Regular bus services connect Goa to nearby cities and states.

Overall Travelling Expenses:

  • Accommodation: Budget hotels range from ₹1,500 to ₹4,000 per night. Mid-range hotels cost between ₹5,000 to ₹8,000 per night, while luxury hotels can go upwards of ₹12,000 per night.
  • Food: Casual dining can cost around ₹300 to ₹1,000 per meal. Mid-range restaurants might charge between ₹1,500 to ₹3,000 for a meal for two, while fine dining can exceed ₹4,000.
  • Transportation: Local transport includes taxis, auto-rickshaws, and rental bikes/cars.
  • Activities: Entry fees to attractions and charges for activities such as river cruises, water sports, and sightseeing. Budget for around ₹1,500 to ₹4,000 per day for activities.
